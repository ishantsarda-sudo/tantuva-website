About TANTUVA

Our Story

TANTUVA represents the essence of Indian heritage and craftsmanship. Born from a deep appreciation for our cultural roots, particularly the rich textile traditions of Assam, we celebrate the traditional art of kurta-making, blending centuries-old techniques with modern design sensibilities. Each piece tells a story of dedication, skill, and cultural pride.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to preserve and promote India's magnificent handloom heritage while creating contemporary fashion that resonates with today's discerning customers. From the handwoven silk of Sualkuchi to the finest cotton from across India, every fabric we select carries with it generations of artisan expertise and traditional knowledge.

We work directly with master weavers and skilled craftspeople, ensuring fair wages and sustainable livelihoods for artisan communities. From classic cotton perfect for everyday elegance to luxurious Assamese silk adorned with traditional gold zari work for special occasions, our collections honor regional traditions while embracing contemporary fashion.

At TANTUVA, we believe in sustainable practices, ethical sourcing, and creating garments that transcend generations. We offer personalized customization - from monograms to bespoke embroidery patterns and unique button designs - making each kurta a reflection of your individual style. Every stitch, every thread, every detail is carefully considered to deliver not just clothing, but heirloom pieces that celebrate your heritage and express your identity.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does customization work? +

After placing your order, our team will contact you within 24 hours to discuss your customization preferences including button style, embroidery patterns, monogram placement, and any special requests. We'll provide samples and mockups before beginning the crafting process.

What is the delivery time? +

Regular orders are delivered within 5-7 business days. Customized orders may take 10-14 days depending on the complexity of embroidery and design. We ensure careful packaging and timely delivery.

How do I choose the right size? +

Our kurtas are available in sizes S, M, L, XL, and XXL. We recommend measuring your shoulder width, chest, and length. If you need assistance, please contact our customer service team for a size guide.

What is your return and exchange policy? +

We offer a 7-day return/exchange policy for unused items in original packaging. Customized items cannot be returned but can be exchanged for defects. Contact our support team to initiate the process.

How should I care for my kurta? +

Dry clean silk kurtas for longevity. Cotton kurtas can be gently hand-washed in cool water with mild soap. Avoid direct sunlight while drying. Store in a cool, dry place. Do not bleach or use harsh chemicals.

Do you ship Internationally? +

Currently, we ship within India only. However, we're expanding our reach and plan to offer international shipping soon. Contact us for special requests and we'll do our best to accommodate.
